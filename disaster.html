Current Disasters

know about some current disasters you can help with
Himalayan floods

Floods and landslides on August 14 in the Indian Himalayan Region killed at least 72 people.Losses were estimated at $1.2 billion, and several hundred roads were closed. Portions of Himachal Pradesh received rainfall amounts of more than 12 inches

Odisha Train Collision

On 2 June 2023, three trains collided in Balasore district in Odisha state of eastern India. A total of 296 people were killed in the crash and more than 1,200 others were injured. It was India's deadliest railway crash since the Firozabad rail collision in 1995

Uttarakhand

On 12 November 2023 a portion of the Silkyara-Dandalgaon tunnel being built in Uttarkashi collapsed after a landslide, trapping 41 labourers behind 57 metres of debris. The 400-hour rescue operation, culminated with 14 “rat-hole miners” successfully digging through the last but toughest 12 metres.

Sikkim Floods

On October 4, a cloudburst in the glacial South Lhonak Lake of North Sikkim triggered a flash flood in Teesta. The raging river left at least 42 people dead and thousands displaced, inundating villages and towns.

Nagpur Flood

The 2023 Nagpur Flood was a flooding event that occurred in the city of Nagpur in the Indian State of Maharashtra on September 24, 2023. The flood caused deaths and economic destruction with several hundred people being evacuated in various parts of the city.

Thathri land subsidence

On 1 February 2023, a land subsidence event developed in the town of Thathri in the Doda district of Jammu and Kashmir, India, resulting in 23 structures being declared unsafe and approximately 300 people being displaced.
